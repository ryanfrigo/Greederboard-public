In 2018, 3.45 billion U.S. dollars were spent on lobbying.

Lobbying represents the interests of the largest corporations and is driven primarily by corporate greed.

Since this is a legal form of corruption, it's impossible to hold businesses and politicians accountable for what they say and how they affect the future of humanity.

Greederboard aims to offer a free and unbiased platform for the American voter to explore the relationships between politicians, lobbyists, corporations, and our legislation.

Our mission is to deliver transparency to the American voter by providing metrics based only on public financial and political data.

We hope that Greederboard can help people see through the flurry of media and derive a more accurate picture of our political system in real time.
